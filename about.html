About Us



GeoTech Cafe invites you to experience a world of cautiously crafted culinary delights right in the heart of your software development haven.

From freshly brewed artisanal coffee to mouthwatering gourmet sandwiches, from the all time favorite fried version of the 2 min noodles, to healthy snacks like upma or oats, our cafe is designed to supercharge your productivity and satisfy your taste buds.

Join us for a coding break that's as sumptuous as your code – because great software deserves great food. Discover the perfect blend of tech and taste at GeoTech Cafe !
